What We Do

At Kezan, we invite you to indulge in relaxation within the serene confines of our cozy residential-based spa. Our passionate owner and sole practitioner, brings her expertise to every treatment. Here’s what we offer:

Massage

Our skilled hands work magic, easing away tension and promoting overall well-being. Whether you seek a soothing Swedish massage or a rejuvenating deep tissue session, we tailor each experience to your unique needs.

Pedicure

Treat your feet to a pampering pedicure. From nail shaping and cuticle care to luxurious foot massages, we’ll have you stepping out with confidence.

Dermal planing

Experience the ultimate exfoliation with our dermal planing service. Say goodbye to dull skin as we gently remove dead cells, revealing a radiant complexion.

Facial

Our customized facials are a blend of science and relaxation. Let us analyze your skin and design a treatment that leaves you glowing. Whether it’s hydration, anti-aging, or acne control, we’ve got you covered.

WHY KEZANE ?

At Kezane, we’re passionate about creating a haven of relaxation right in the heart of  our Spa.

Holistic Wellness: Beyond massages and facials, we believe in holistic well-being. Our treatments are designed to nurture your mind, body, and spirit. Whether it’s a grounding aromatherapy massage or a chakra-balancing facial, we’re here to restore your inner harmony.

Personalized Experiences: No two clients are alike, and neither are our treatments. We take the time to understand your unique needs and preferences. From selecting the perfect essential oils to customizing pressure during massages, your comfort is our priority.

Hygiene and Safety: Your health matters to us. We adhere to strict hygiene protocols, ensuring sanitized tools, fresh linens, and a clean, serene space. Trust us to provide a safe haven where you can unwind without worry.

Eco-Friendly Practices: As stewards of both wellness and the environment, we use eco-friendly and cruelty-free products. Our commitment extends to sustainable practices within our spa room. Relax guilt-free, knowing that your self-care aligns with our planet’s well-being.
